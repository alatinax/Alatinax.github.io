Frequently Asked Questions

What types of insurance do you offer?

We specialize in both personal and commercial insurance including homeowners, auto, general liability, and more.

How do I request a quote?

You can request a quote by visiting our Contact section or by submitting the form directly on the Personal or Commercial pages.

Are there any fees for getting a quote?

Nope! All quotes provided by Florida State Insurance Group are 100% free and personalized to your needs.

What documents do I need to get started?

Basic information such as your name, address, and the property or vehicle details are typically enough to begin. We'll guide you step-by-step after that.

Do you offer bundle discounts?

Yes! We can bundle homeowners, auto, and umbrella policies to help you save on premiums while expanding coverage.

Can I switch insurance providers anytime?

Absolutely — and we make it seamless. We’ll help you cancel your old policy and activate your new one without gaps in coverage.

What areas do you serve?

We serve clients across the state of Florida, including Broward, Miami-Dade, Palm Beach, and beyond.
