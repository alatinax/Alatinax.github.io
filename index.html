About Me

With several years of experience in the mortgage and real estate industry, I’ve developed a deep understanding of the documentation, compliance, and customer service standards that today’s clients expect. As a former Office Manager and Administrative Assistant at a local mortgage firm, I worked closely with loan officers, underwriters, and clients to streamline application processes and ensure files were accurate and audit-ready.

My attention to detail, familiarity with financial documentation, and hands-on experience all support my current role in the insurance industry. I bring this same precision and professionalism to every client interaction—whether I’m reviewing policy details, helping you find the right coverage, or guiding you through the quoting process.

Now, as a licensed Florida 2-20 insurance agent, I specialize in personal and commercial lines of insurance. My approach is simple: treat every client like family. I believe in building trust through clear communication, responsive service, and tailored insurance solutions that fit your life and goals—not just a policy number.
